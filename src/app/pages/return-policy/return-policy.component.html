Return Policy

Last updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

This Return Policy explains how Panchajanya Textiles handles claims for goods that arrive damaged, defective, or incorrect. It should be read together with our Terms & Conditions. As a wholesale and bulk distributor of home textiles, uniforms, and fabrics, our policy is designed around business-to-business orders rather than individual retail purchases.

Return Request Window

Any claim for damaged, defective, or incorrect goods must be raised within 48 hours of receiving your order, by writing to us at panchajanya.madurai@gmail.com (Madurai) or panchajanya.chennai@gmail.com (Chennai), or by calling the office nearest you. Claims raised after this window may not be accepted.

Once a claim is raised, we aim to review and process your request within 7 business days, and will keep you informed of its status throughout.

Refund & Exchange Options

Where a claim is approved, we will resolve it through one of the following, at our discretion:

  • a product replacement of the same or equivalent item; or
  • a credit note to be adjusted against a future order.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a replacement or credit note, your claim must meet the following conditions:

  • the goods show a visible defect or damage in terms of quality, colour, or size;
  • the goods are unused and in their original condition; and
  • all original packaging, labels, and accompanying documentation (such as the invoice) are complete and intact, and supporting evidence (photographs and invoice) is provided with the claim.

Goods showing signs of use, damage, staining, or alteration that were not reported at the time of the claim will not be eligible for replacement or credit.

Important Notes

Colour Variations

Slight differences in colour due to lighting, photography, dye lots, or screen settings are natural and will not be treated as defects.

Custom & Institutional Orders

Customised, personalised, or special-order products, including uniforms and fabrics made to an institution's specific requirements, are produced especially for you and are not eligible for return or replacement, except where they arrive damaged or defective.

Improper Handling

Goods damaged through improper use, handling, storage, or washing after delivery will not qualify for return or replacement.

Change of Preference

Goods sold cannot be returned solely on account of a change of preference once dispatched.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Return Policy or to raise a claim, please reach out to us at panchajanya.madurai@gmail.com (Madurai) or panchajanya.chennai@gmail.com (Chennai), or via the details on our Contact page.
